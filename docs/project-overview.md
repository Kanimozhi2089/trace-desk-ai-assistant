# Trace Desk AI Requirement Assistant

## 1. Project Overview

### Business Problem

Business requirements are often incomplete, unclear, or inconsistent. 
This creates challenges between Business Analysts, Product Owners, Developers, and QA teams, leading to clarification cycles, requirement gaps, rework, and delayed delivery.

### Target Users

Primary users:

- Business Analysts
- QA Engineers
- Product Owners
- Development Teams

### Solution Approach

Trace Desk is an AI-powered requirement analysis assistant that helps teams improve requirement quality by analyzing business requirements and generating structured BA and QA artifacts.

The assistant can:

- Identify requirement gaps and ambiguities
- Generate clarification questions
- Create user stories
- Generate acceptance criteria
- Create test scenarios and test cases
- Improve requirement traceability between business needs and QA validation

### Benefits

- Improves requirement clarity
- Reduces communication gaps between teams
- Reduces requirement rework
- Accelerates BA documentation activities
- Supports faster QA test preparation
- Improves end-to-end requirement traceability

---

## 2. Project Goals

The goal of Trace Desk is to demonstrate how AI can support Business Analysts and QA professionals throughout the software development lifecycle.
